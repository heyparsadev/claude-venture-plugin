# Messaging House Template

Write in the **customers' language** (may differ from report language — a Persian product with Persian buyers gets Persian messaging even in an English report; show both).

```markdown
# Messaging House — <Venture>
<date> · audience: <ICP from positioning §4>

## Roof — the one promise
<Single sentence, customer outcome, no jargon. The thing they'd repeat to a colleague.
e.g. «دیگه هیچ نوبتی سوخت نمی‌شه» — not "AI-powered appointment optimization platform">

## Pillars (3 — each an outcome, not a feature)

### Pillar 1: <outcome>
- Proof: <feature/mechanism that delivers it>
- Proof: <number, testimonial, or demo moment — real ones only; TBD if none yet>

### Pillar 2: <outcome>
- Proof: ...

### Pillar 3: <outcome>
- Proof: ...

## Objections & responses
| They think | We say |
|---|---|
| "<top objection from discovery/reviews>" | <response, honest> |
| "<price objection>" | <value reframe> |
| "<switching-cost objection>" | <migration/onboarding answer> |

## Message → asset map
| Asset | Leads with |
|---|---|
| Homepage hero | Roof |
| Ad copy / cold outreach opener | Pillar with sharpest pain for that channel |
| Sales deck | Roof → pillars in order → objections |
| App store / marketplace listing | Roof + social proof |

## Words bank
- **Customers' own words** (verbatim from interviews/reviews — use these, they convert): <...>
- **Words to avoid** (jargon, claims we can't prove, competitor-owned terms): <...>
```
