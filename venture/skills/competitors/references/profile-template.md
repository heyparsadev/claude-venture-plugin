# Competitor Profile Template

Filled per competitor (by competitor-scout agent or inline). Every claim cited; estimates carry confidence (high/med/low).

```markdown
# Competitor Profile: <Name>
<date> · category: <direct | indirect | substitute>

## Snapshot
- **What they do:** <one paragraph — their framing, then your neutral framing>
- **Founded / HQ / size:** <year, location, headcount estimate (source)>
- **Funding / ownership:** <rounds, investors, or bootstrapped/unknown (source)>
- **Target customer:** <who they actually serve — infer from pricing, copy, case studies>

## Product
- **Core features:** <5–8 that matter, not the whole list>
- **Notably missing / weak:** <gaps relevant to our venture's ICP>
- **Platform & integrations:** <web/mobile/API; ecosystem position>

## Pricing
| Plan | Price | What's gated |
|---|---|---|
<from pricing page; if hidden, note "contact sales" and any reported figures (source). Check archive.org for recent pricing changes.>

## Traction signals
<users/customers claimed (their claim — mark as such), app installs bracket, review counts
& velocity, web traffic estimate, notable customers. Each with source + confidence.>

## Go-to-market
- **Primary channels:** <how customers find them — SEO, ads, sales team, partners, marketplace>
- **Positioning & message:** <their headline promise; who they position against>

## Voice of their customers
- **Loved (from 4–5★ reviews):** <patterns, with 1–2 short quotes>
- **Hated (from 1–2★ reviews):** <patterns, with 1–2 short quotes — this is the gap map>
- Sources: <G2/Capterra/app stores/Trustpilot/forums — links>

## Recent moves
<last ~12 months: launches, pricing changes, funding, layoffs, pivots (sources)>

## Threat assessment (relative to our venture)
- **Threat level:** <high/med/low> — <why, in 2–3 sentences>
- **Where they'd beat us:** <...>
- **Where we can beat them:** <...>

## Sources
<numbered list with URLs and dates>
```
