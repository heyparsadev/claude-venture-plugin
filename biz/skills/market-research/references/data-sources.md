# Data Sources Guide

## Universal rules

1. **Local market → local language.** Search Persian for Iran, Turkish for Turkey, Arabic for Gulf. English-only queries undercount local markets badly. Translate key findings back into the report language.
2. **Prefer primary over aggregator:** a statistics bureau beats a blog quoting it. Chase the original.
3. Note **what a number measures** (revenue vs users vs downloads vs bookings) and **which year**.
4. Paywalled report? Its **press release / executive summary** usually carries the headline numbers free — search `"<report name>" press release`.

## By question

| Question | Where to look |
|---|---|
| Industry size & growth | Statista, Grand View Research, Mordor, IBISWorld (press summaries); national statistics bureaus; industry association annual reports |
| Number of businesses (B2B sizing) | business registries, chamber-of-commerce stats, professional licensing bodies, LinkedIn company-count filters |
| Consumer behavior & demographics | national census/statistics portals, World Bank / OECD data, Google Trends (relative demand & seasonality) |
| Startup/competitor activity & funding | Crunchbase, Dealroom, TechCrunch, local startup media |
| App/product demand | Google Play / App Store rankings & install brackets, review counts and velocity, similarweb (web traffic) |
| Pricing in market | competitor pricing pages (archive.org for history), marketplace listings, reseller/agency rate cards |
| Regulation | the actual regulator's site, law-firm client briefings |

## Iran-specific (use Persian queries)

| Question | Sources |
|---|---|
| Demographics & macro | مرکز آمار ایران (amar.org.ir), بانک مرکزی reports |
| Digital/app market | Cafe Bazaar developer stats, Digikala trend reports (دیجی‌کالا گزارش), Techrasa/Digiato/Zoomit market coverage |
| Startups & funding | گزارش‌های صندوق‌های VC داخلی, ایران‌وی‌سی/Smartup coverage, جشنواره‌ها و انجمن‌های صنفی |
| Businesses count | اتحادیه‌ها و نظام‌های صنفی (e.g. نظام پزشکی for clinics), وزارت‌خانه‌های مربوطه |

Sanctions caveat: many global datasets exclude or undercount Iran — flag this and lean on local sources.

## Confidence rubric (apply to every figure)

- **high** — ≥2 independent credible sources agree (±20%), recent.
- **med** — single credible source, or older data, or strong proxy.
- **low** — weak proxy, extrapolation, or conflicting sources. Low-confidence numbers may inform but never anchor the recommendation.
